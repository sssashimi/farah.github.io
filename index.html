About Us

Airbending, one of the four elemental bending arts, is the aerokinetic ability to control and manipulate air. Air is an element of freedom. The peaceful Air Nomads utilized this type of bending in their everyday lives. The first airbenders learned their art from the flying bison.


The key to airbending is flexibility and finding and following the path of least resistance. Airbending is notable for being almost purely defensive, as well as the most dynamic of the four bending arts. Airbenders can overwhelm many opponents at once with large and powerful attacks that could prove fatal; however, due to the pacifist nature of the Air Nomads, such attacks are rarely used.
